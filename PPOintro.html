Pharmaceutical Manufacturing Outsourcing Optimization

Background

This project seeks to approach a common pharmaceutical industry challenge of scaling a drug to production after FDA approval. There is urgency in the process of scaling to production because after filing, the patent for the drug is only held by the company for 10 years and vertically integrating is no longer the most economically viable option. 3rd party vendors must be employed to sequentially build the molecule over many steps that results in the active pharmaceutical ingredient (API). Selection of these vendors can be a strenous and inefficient process to work out as dozens of steps is common to build an API, however if each vendor is modeled by a set of parameters, computational optimization is the perfect tool to rapidly determine the third party vendor sequence for API construction that allows for the pharma company to realize the maximum profit the patent is able to produce within the 10 year period.
